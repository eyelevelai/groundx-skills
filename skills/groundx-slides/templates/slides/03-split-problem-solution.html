THE PROBLEM

Generic RAG misses what matters.

Enterprise documents aren't prose — they're tables, forms, nested captions, and diagrams. A text-first ingestion pipeline flattens all of that and your model retrieves the wrong thing 4 out of 10 queries.

THE ANSWER

Ingest like a person reads.

GroundX parses the structure of the document first — tables as tables, captions bound to figures, sections preserving their hierarchy — then builds retrieval around that structure. The model gets what it asked for.
