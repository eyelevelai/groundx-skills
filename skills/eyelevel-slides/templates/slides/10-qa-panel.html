YOUR QUESTIONS · OUR RESPONSES

Direct answers, point by point.

DIFFERENTIATION · CAPABILITIES

What can't be replicated with a standard RAG pipeline?

A vision model fine-tuned to identify graphics, tables, and paragraphs; agentic processing of document elements coupled with proprietary hybrid search; deployment in the highest-security environments — including fully on-prem.

SCOPE · FULL PIPELINE

Retrieval only, or a full RAG pipeline including generation?

Complete solution. Document understanding, retrieval with hybrid search at million-page scale, and response generation — connect any LLM, including self-hosted and air-gapped models.

STRUCTURED DATA · CRITICAL

How are tables and charts represented after ingestion?

Both — structured (JSON) and unstructured (narrative) — simultaneously. Dual representation improves search performance and LLM completions, and can be customized to any output schema.

RETRIEVAL MODEL · SEMANTIC OBJECTS

What exactly are you retrieving — text or structured objects?

Not text chunks. GroundX turns documents into structured, LLM-ready representations and retrieves both semantic text and the underlying structure — tables, images, sections, key-value data — with relationships preserved.
